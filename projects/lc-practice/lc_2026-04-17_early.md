### 摆动排序 (LC280/324)

### 二维前缀和 (LC304)
- prefix[i+1][j+1] = grid[i][j] + prefix[i][j+1] + prefix[i+1][j] - prefix[i][j]
- 区域和: sumRegion(r1,c1,r2,c2) = prefix[r2+1][c2+1] - prefix[r1][c2+1] - prefix[r2+1][c1] + prefix[r1][c1]
